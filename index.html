Welcome to the Exploring Technologies for Teaching, Learning and Assessment.

On the Exploring Technology pages are a place for us to share ideas, knowledge and questions about:

  • different technologies and how they can support different types of learning, teaching and assessment activities
  • institutionally supported technologies and others available on the open web
  • examples of how these technologies are used, including discussing potential benefits, drawbacks and limitations
  • key considerations when designing and using specific technologies
  • investigating further help and support, and planning next steps.
